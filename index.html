About me

Front End Developer | Multimedia Production

I have spent most of my life working in visual communications, either on commercial printing presses, in pre-press, or as a production artist. About 8 years ago, I decided it was time for a change, so I went back to school to get a degree in multimedia at Portland Community College. While I was there, I discovered web development and knew this was what I wanted to do. After graduation, I enrolled in the frontend web development program at Seattle University. I spent a year at Seattle University and transferred to Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago. I will be finishing that program in January of 2021. I am always taking supplemental online classes on Udemy or Lynda.com in order to learn more and refine my skills. I also do free websites periodically to learn more and have portfolio material. I enjoy the tediousness and focus of coding, it provides me with a sanctuary from the rest of the world.
